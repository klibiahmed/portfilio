To enable the "Download Resume" button on your portfolio:

1. Add your resume PDF file to this folder (public/)
2. Name it: resume.pdf
3. That's it! The button will work automatically.

Example:
  c:\Users\Ahmed\Desktop\Portfolio\public\resume.pdf

The button is located in the Hero section, right below your description.

For more details, see: RESUME_SETUP.md in the root folder.

Note: You can delete this file after adding your resume.
